The Biodiversity of The Aegean
Greek Authority Turkish Authority NGOs
The Aegean Sea is home to more than 10.000 species of marine life.
This cerulean blue ecosystem is the home of many unique endemic species (such as the cute monk seal you see here) as well as globe-trotting cosmopolitan species. The preservation of the biodiversity in this region is key for a better future concerning the wider Mediterranean.
